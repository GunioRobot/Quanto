Quanto!

A project to profile your queries as they hit a mysql server. There's a small mysql-proxy script written in lua which peeks at all your queries as they go by, then peeks at the result as they come back.

You generate query comments for each of your queries, and Quanto stores aggregate query statistics, such as timing information, call counts, rows returned, etc.

Then there's some CGI written in perl and using a tiny amount of JQuery to retrive the stats and display the results nicely.
