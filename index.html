Quanto!

A project to profile your queries as they hit a mysql server. There's a small mysql-proxy script written in lua which peeks at all your queries as they go by, then peeks at the result as they come back. Then there's some CGI written in perl and using a tiny amount of JQuery to display the results nicely.
